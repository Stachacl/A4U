Artists4Ukraine

ВИСТАВКА МИСТЕЦТВ

English text: Artist4Ukraine is a charity exhibition with the aim to raise funds for Ukrainian children that have suffered as a result of the ongoing war.

All artworks have been generously donated by leading Australian artists, with 100% of the proceeds from sales to be donated to The Voices of Children Foundation (www.voices.org.ua)

Your support of the Artists4Ukraine exhibition will make a difference to the lives of the thousands of Children and families assisted by Voices of Children Foundation every year.

About The Voices of Children Foundation: The Voices of Children Foundation is an organisation that has been helping children affected by war since 2015. The Voices of Children Foundation are assisting with the evacuation of families with children, finding safe housing for them, providing them with food, clothing, and much needed psychological support.

Due to the volume of artworks donated some of the artworks are available online only. Please visit our The Online Exhibition to see these works.

Artworks can be purchased, onsite and online. To buy the artwork please fill the form on the Contact Page.

Together we can make a difference in the lives of children affected by the traumas of war.
