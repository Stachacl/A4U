Artists4Ukraine

ВИСТАВКА МИСТЕЦЬТВ

We (a team of art curators from Meadowbank TAFE) are endeavouring to have an awareness and fundraising event planned for May 19 2022. Funds and awareness for this event will go towards addressing urgent children's medical needs as well as the disabled and psychologically tortured population of Ukraine. This is an area of need that deserves immediate attention and should be focused on right now, if there is to be any possibility of world peace into the foreseeable future. We have invited and are curating an exhibition that a large number of prominent artists have donated their high quality work towards, and feel that good quality media coverage would be an advantage towards achieving this goal. Of the 50 or so artists who have responded with donations of works of art are: Gary Shead, Wendy Sharpe, Seraphina Martin, Peter Kingston, Lucy Culliton and George Gittoes. This event will be on site at -: See Street Gallery, Meadowbank TAFE. Meadowbank and a on-line at www.artists4Ukraine.com.au 100% of funds raised will be donated to https://voices.org.ua/
